About Me

From building computers out of recycled parts with my dad as a kid, I’ve always been drawn to creating things that make life easier. My journey into tech started early, and I found my way into marketing as a gateway to technology roles. Over time, I carved out a niche as a Marketing Technology Specialist, where I thrive on bridging the gap between people and technology.

A pivotal moment in my career came when I was tasked with QA for a large-scale web redesign. I developed a system to scan and check thousands of pages in multiple languages, streamlining the process and opening doors to more impactful projects. Since then, I’ve focused on building intuitive tools and automations that make teams more efficient—like a web scraping tool that saved my company $500k a year and is still in use years later.

My approach is simple: start with the end goal, work backwards, and always keep the user’s needs front and center. I focus on driving projects to completion by meticulously organizing requirements and tenaciously seeking solutions, even when the path isn’t immediately clear. In my collaborations, I aim to be proactive and organized, fostering a positive team environment where, I believe, a bit of humor and kindness can go a long way.

Outside of work, I enjoy time with my family, tackling home projects, and helping others with technology. I’m passionate about continuous learning, especially in areas like AI and client-facing web development, and I believe the best results come from teams that collaborate, learn, and grow together.
